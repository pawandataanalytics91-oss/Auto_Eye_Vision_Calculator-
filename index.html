Frequently Asked Questions

What does 6/6 vision mean?
6/6 vision is considered normal visual acuity. It means you can see at 6 meters what a person with normal vision sees at 6 meters. This is equivalent to 20/20 vision in the imperial system.
How often should I get my eyes checked?
Adults should have a comprehensive eye exam every 1-2 years. Children should have their first eye exam at 6 months, then at 3 years, before first grade, and every 1-2 years thereafter.
Can vision impairment be reversed?
Many causes of vision impairment can be treated or corrected with glasses, contact lenses, medication, or surgery. However, some conditions may cause permanent vision loss. Early detection is key.
What is the difference between OD and OS?
OD (Oculus Dexter) refers to the right eye, OS (Oculus Sinister) refers to the left eye, and OU (Oculus Uterque) means both eyes. These are standard medical abbreviations used in eye care.
